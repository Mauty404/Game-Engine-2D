Q - draw all
E - clear all

R - change bitmap to red
T - change bitmap to green
Y - change bitmap to blue
U - change bitmap to black

I - draw primitives
O - draw line incremental
G - draw line by two points

H - draw circle & elipse
J - draw empty primitive
K - boundary fill (crash program)
L - flood fill (crash program)

Z - draw line
X - scale line (0,0)
C - scale line (900, 0)
V - rotate line (0,0)
B - rotate line (600, 600)
C - translate line
